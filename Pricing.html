Frequently asked questions



What is Knolgo?

Knolgo is an amazing place for learning and fun. It's a website where you can join clubs, embark on quests, and participate in interactive pods. It's designed to make learning easy, exciting, and enjoyable!

Is Knolgo suitable for all ages and levels of learners?

Knolgo is designed for K - 12 Learners.

How does Quest work?

Quests are exciting adventures that take you on a learning journey. You complete missions and earn rewards. They're like fun challenges that help you learn new things while having a great time.

How does Clubs work?

Clubs on Knolgo are like groups where you can explore your favorite topics. You join a club, participate in discussions, and learn together. It's a great way to meet people who share your interests and expand your knowledge.

How does Learning Pod work?

A Learning Pod is like a small virtual classroom where you and other learners come together to explore a specific topic or subject.
